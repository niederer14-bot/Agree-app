Onboarding and Navigation

First launch
1. Everyone has an opinion.
2. Nobody can decide.
3. Agree. Decide what to eat or watch in under two minutes.

Then:
- username
- DOB / age gate
- optional photo
- location permission explanation
- taste setup swipe cards

Taste setup
- minimum 20 answers
- user can continue beyond 20
- mix of eat + watch prompts
- back arrow undo allowed only here

Main home screen
- two large cinematic choices:
  - Agree on what to eat
  - Agree on what to watch

Bottom center ring
- tap = start decision from current mode/context
- press and hold = open radial actions
