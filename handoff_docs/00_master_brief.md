AGREE — Master Brief

AGREE is a social decision engine that helps people agree on what to eat or what to watch in under two minutes.

Core promise
Stop debating. Agree.

Launch scope
- Agree on what to eat
- Agree on what to watch

Core interaction
- full-screen swipe cards
- group sessions
- private circles
- percentage-based taste matching
- single winner + one backup
- result sharing by text or Facebook

Primary audience
- ages 22–35
- young professionals
- couples
- roommates
- friend groups

Product posture
- utility first
- social mechanics second
- free product
- no paid tiers at launch
